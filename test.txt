Obelix has gone to the jungle to catch a wild boar but the romans are trying to capture him for destroying their garrison.

Help Obelix to catch the wild boar by dodging the roman soldiers. 

Press the up arrow key to jump and dodge the soldiers.

Be careful as you have only three lives!

Collect the meat pieces to earn points.

Press the space key to do a small jump to collect the meat.
